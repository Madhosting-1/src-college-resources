About the Trip

Belur and Halebidu, located in the Hassan district of Karnataka, are home to some of the most intricate examples of Hoysala architecture. This educational trip aims to provide an in-depth understanding of the region's rich cultural and architectural heritage. Students and history enthusiasts alike will have the opportunity to study the art, craftsmanship, and religious significance of these historic temples.

Did You Know?

The Chennakesava Temple at Belur is famous for its detailed carvings depicting scenes from Hindu mythology, while the Hoysaleswara Temple at Halebidu is an architectural marvel built entirely without mortar.
